F.A.Q.

Who decides the selling price?

You do. You're the boss. Some publishers want a token donation of $0.99 from listeners; others make their training available as a private audio course for $97/mo. You can experiment with special offers or raise/lower your selling price any time.

Can I have a playlist (or podcast series) that has both free and premium episodes?

Yes. In fact, this is a smart strategy, as it means your playlist can be publicly listed in the iTunes podcast directory: The free episodes will be publicly accessible to all listeners, on any app, from any device. The playlist description will automatically contain a link to purchase full access. The premium episodes will only appear and be accessible to listeners who have purchased full access to this playlist.

Do listeners have to download yet another app?

No, we've our solution works with all existing podcast apps, and is ideal for use with the pre-installed iOS app, Pocket Casts, Google Play Music and more. In addition, listeners can access your content via your profile on Awesound.

Do I have to sign an exclusivity agreement?

No, we've more respect for creators than that! We hope that you enjoy publishing paid content with Awesound thanks to our technology helping you better understand your listeners, because we help new fans to discover you and because you profit with very competitive rates. We don't lock you in; you can simultaneously sell your content anywhere else you like, or leave Awesound any time.

Who will promote my podcast/audiobook/music?

Your audiobook or premium podcast may be featured in Awesound's "publisher spotlight" or other promotional activity carried out by Awesound, but the primary responsibility for marketing your content is up to you.

How do I get paid?

As soon as some purchases access, you will receive an email notification and the activity will appear in your Awesound payments dashboard. You can choose to withdraw your balance manually any time, or choose to be paid automatically periodically (e.g., weekly or monthly deposits).

What currency can I charge in?

We currently support USD, EUR and GBP. If you wish to sell in any other currency, please contact us.

How is this is different to Audible?

You'll earn two or three times more with Awesound. (Audible/ACX keeps a cheeky 60-75% of what listeners pay!) You'll also be more in control of pricing and branding, so you can experiment with what works for you.
In addition, you get rich analytics on who's listening to your content, so you can understand and grow your audience.

How is this is different to Patreon?

We help you better control your relationship with the listeners who are supporting you: you can let listeners access content on your own website, and you can see the email address for every customer who buys your content. (This is very important if you wish to contact patrons directly, or switch platform in future.)
Furthermore, we let you fully control the selling price your supporter actually pays: we don't slap on any surprise "payment processing fee". Learn more.

How can I try this out?

Simply sign up for free and choose what price you want to charge for your content! Questions? Email team@awesound.com or ask us on Twitter or Facebook.
